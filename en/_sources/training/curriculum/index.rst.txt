Curriculum and Guide for Facilitator
====================================

This section contains the standard curriculum and facilitator guide for 
the |project_name| training course designed by Badan Nasional Penanggulangan 
Bencana (BNPB), Humanitarian OpenStreetMap Team (HOT) and Australia-Indonesia 
Facility for Disaster Reduction (AIFDR). The training course consists of four
units which provide hands-on experience with |project_name|, |QGIS| and |OSM|.

The training modules to be used with this curriculum are available 
in :ref:`beginner` and :ref:`intermediate`.
