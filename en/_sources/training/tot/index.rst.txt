Training of Trainer Module
==========================

The main objective of this training is to train people to be leaders and
trainers in Scenario Development program for Contingency Planning (SD4CP) in their
respective regions. After completing the training, participants will have the
facilitation skills in OSM / QGIS / InaSAFE which are needed as a facilitator,
and training skills to help train others in developing a realistic disaster
scenario that can be used in contingency plans. This will lead to better
decision-making during the process of making contingency plans. It is also 
expected that TOT participants can provide technical assistance in their 
respective regions.

The course is scheduled to run for one day.
